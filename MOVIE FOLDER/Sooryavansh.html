Story Line:

Sooryavansh is an action-packed drama that follows the story of a valiant hero, Soorya, played by Anubhav Mohanty, who is determined to bring justice and protect his community from the clutches of powerful and corrupt forces. The movie is a blend of intense action sequences, emotional moments, and a gripping narrative that keeps the audience engaged throughout. Soorya's journey is filled with challenges as he confronts the villains and fights for the truth, making it a thrilling and inspiring watch.
