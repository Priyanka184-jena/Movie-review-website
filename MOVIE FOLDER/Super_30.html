Story Line:

Super 30 (2019) follows the inspiring true story of Anand Kumar, a brilliant mathematician from a modest background in Bihar, India. Anand dreams of providing underprivileged students with the opportunity to pursue higher education and compete for a spot at the prestigious Indian Institutes of Technology (IITs). Despite facing financial difficulties and societal obstacles, Anand establishes the "Super 30" program, a coaching class dedicated to training 30 talented but impoverished students for the highly competitive IIT entrance exams. The film portrays Anand's journey, highlighting his dedication, perseverance, and the transformative impact he has on his students' lives. As Anand faces opposition from powerful forces and corruption within the educational system, he remains steadfast in his mission. The movie showcases the challenges and triumphs of both Anand and his students, ultimately emphasizing the power of education and the potential to overcome adversity through determination and hard work.
