Genres: Crime, Drama, Thriller

Cast:

  2. Tabu
  3. Shriya Saran
  4. Akshaye Khanna

Release Date:November 18, 2022

Duration:142 minutes


IMDb Rating:8.3/10

Story Line:

Seven years after the events of the first film, Vijay Salgaonkar (Ajay Devgn) and his family have settled into their new lives. Vijay has fulfilled his dream of owning a cinema hall and is now working on producing a movie. However, the past resurfaces when new evidence threatens to uncover the truth behind the mysterious disappearance of Sam, the son of Inspector General Meera Deshmukh (Tabu). The family faces new challenges as they strive to protect their secrets, and Vijay must use his wit and resourcefulness once again to outmaneuver the authorities and keep his family safe. The film explores themes of guilt, redemption, and the lengths one will go to protect their loved ones.
