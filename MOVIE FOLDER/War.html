War (2019)

Genres:Action, Thriller
Director:Siddharth Anand
Cast:
  1. Hrithik Roshan
  2. Tiger Shroff
  3. Vaani Kapoor
  4. Ashutosh Rana
Release Date:October 2, 2019
Duration:154 minutes


IMDb Rating:6.5/10

Story Line:

War follows the story of an Indian soldier, Khalid (Tiger Shroff), who is assigned to eliminate his former mentor and Indian intelligence agent, Kabir (Hrithik Roshan), who has gone rogue. The film takes the audience on a thrilling journey across multiple countries with high-octane action sequences, jaw-dropping stunts, and an intense cat-and-mouse chase. The narrative weaves through various twists and turns, keeping viewers on the edge of their seats as Khalid uncovers the true motives behind Kabir's actions. The story also delves into themes of loyalty, betrayal, and patriotism, making "War" a compelling and entertaining action thriller.
