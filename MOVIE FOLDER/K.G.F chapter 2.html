IMDb Rating:8.4/10

Story Line:

K.G.F: Chapter 2continues the story of Rocky, who, after establishing himself as the kingpin of the Kolar Gold Fields, must defend his throne against adversaries old and new. As he solidifies his control over the empire, Rocky's enemies conspire to bring him down. Adheera, a formidable and ruthless foe, emerges with the intention of claiming the gold fields for himself. Meanwhile, political forces and the government, represented by Ramika Sen, begin to tighten their grip, making Rocky's position increasingly precarious. The movie showcases Rocky's relentless pursuit of power, his rise from the streets to the throne, and his battle against overwhelming odds to maintain his dominance over K.G.F.
